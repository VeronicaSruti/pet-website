About Pet Shop

Your Trusted Partner in Pet Care Since Years

Welcome to Pet Shop, your trusted pet supply store located in the heart of Baranagar, West Bengal. We understand that pets are family, and they deserve the very best care, nutrition, and love.

At Pet Shop, we pride ourselves on offering high-quality pet products ranging from nutritious dog and cat food to comfortable cages, fun accessories, and essential care products. Our knowledgeable and friendly staff are always ready to help you find exactly what your furry, feathered, or fluffy friend needs.
